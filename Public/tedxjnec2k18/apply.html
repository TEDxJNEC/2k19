Rules and Guidelines for registration

Registration

  • This registration is Non-Refundable. There is no refund for NO SHOWS.
  • This registration is Non-Transferable and is valid for one person only.
  • Cancellation and Rescheduling of registration is not allowed.
  • By Registering and making a payment you agree that all details provided are accurate.
  • All registrations are subject to verification, any discrepancy may result in cancellation of registration.
  • Make sure you carry the ticket details on your phone/printout for verification.
  • All attendees must carry a valid government identity card to attain a conference attendee badge at the venue.

Safety and Security

  • Please cooperate with the private security and police personnel for security checks.
  • To ensure your safety, the organizers reserve the rights to frisk and restrict entry.
  • The consumption of any prohibited substances is strictly banned.
  • Carrying of food items, alcohol, cigarettes, weapons, inflammable and banned substances are strictly prohibited.
  • The organizers do not take responsibility for injuries, loss or theft of any personal belongings of ticket holder.
  • Any individual might be asked to leave the venue without any liability of a refund if there is any behavioral misdemeanor. This decision rests with the conference and venue management and is final and binding.
  • All standard venue and attendee rules are to be adhered to by attendees.

The Event Day

  • Entry into the auditorium will only start 20 mins before the event timing.
  • Venue gates will remain shut during sessions and will not open for entry.
  • Attendees reaching late at the venue will NOT be allowed inside the auditorium while talks are in progress.
  • The attendees must wear their conference badge throughout the conference across all venues.
  • There will be adequate breaks for networking and refreshments. Lunch and tea are included in the ticket price.
  • For any queries with respect to venue logistics, parking, toilets, please reach out to our uniformed volunteers.

Best Practices

  • We encourage you to car pool with other attendees or use public transport, and get to know fellow attendees before the event.
  • We request you not to leave half-finished water bottles lying around the venue.
  • Request you to ensure that the event spaces are kept litter free.
  • During sessions, please keep your mobile phones on silent mode or switched off. A ringing phone is a disturbance to the speaker as well as other attendees.
  • No flash photography and recording of the event is allowed.
  • Maintain silence during speaker talks and performances to get the best out of the talk.
  • Do not hesitate in giving the speaker a standing ovation if their talk moved you.
  • Any movement during the talks and performances are strongly discouraged.
Most importantly, have a great time. We look forward to seeing you there.
